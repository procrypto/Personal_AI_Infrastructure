    10→## Core Concepts
    11→
    12→### How Market Structure Maps to the Indicator
    13→
    14→| Market Concept | Rotations Indicator Signal |
    15→|----------------|---------------------------|
    16→| **Break of Structure (BOS)** | Rotation flip (gray → green or gray → red) |
    17→| **Change of Character (CHoCH)** | Rotation invalidation (green/red → gray) |
    18→| **Swing Failure Pattern (SFP)** | Orange-highlighted levels (wick through, close inside) |
    19→| **Higher High / Lower Low** | Green/red bars confirming continued rotation |
    20→| **Structure Break** | Close beyond rotation level + previous bar's high/low |

    54→## Playbook 1: Trend Continuation (Pullback Entry)
    55→
    56→### The Concept
    57→Trade pullbacks within an established rotation direction. This is the highest probability setup because you're trading WITH the confirmed trend.
    58→
    59→### Entry Rules
    60→
    61→**For Longs:**
    62→1. Bars are GREEN (upward rotation confirmed)
    63→2. Price pulls back toward `rotationLow`
    64→3. Price shows rejection from the level (bullish candle pattern)
    65→4. Enter long on the rejection candle close
    66→
    67→**For Shorts:**
    68→1. Bars are RED (downward rotation confirmed)
    69→2. Price pulls back toward `rotationHigh`
    70→3. Price shows rejection from the level (bearish candle pattern)
    71→4. Enter short on the rejection candle close
    72→
    73→### Stop Loss
    74→- **Longs:** Below `rotationLow` minus 0.5 ATR buffer
    75→- **Shorts:** Above `rotationHigh` plus 0.5 ATR buffer
    76→
    77→### Take Profit
    78→- Previous swing high/low
    79→- 2:1 or 3:1 risk-reward ratio
    80→- Trail using rotation levels as they update
    81→
    82→### Example Flow
    83→
    84→```
    85→1. Rotation confirmed GREEN (bullish)
    86→2. Price rallies, then pulls back
    87→3. Price approaches rotationLow (green circles)
    88→4. Hammer/bullish engulfing forms at level
    89→5. Enter long → Stop below rotationLow → Target previous high
    90→```
    91→
    92→### Confluence Filters
    93→- Higher timeframe also in same rotation direction
    94→- Volume increasing on the bounce
    95→- RSI oversold on the pullback (for longs)
    96→- Price at round number or prior S/R

   100→## Playbook 2: Break of Structure Entry
   101→
   102→### The Concept
   103→Enter immediately when rotation flips direction. You're catching the exact moment market structure changes.
   104→
   105→### Entry Rules
   106→
   107→**For Longs:**
   108→1. Bars flip from GRAY or RED to GREEN
   109→2. This confirms: `close > rotationHigh AND close > high[1]`
   110→3. Enter on the confirmed bar close OR first pullback
   111→4. Stop below the new `rotationLow`
   112→
   113→**For Shorts:**
   114→1. Bars flip from GRAY or GREEN to RED
   115→2. This confirms: `close < rotationLow AND close < low[1]`
   116→3. Enter on the confirmed bar close OR first pullback
   117→4. Stop above the new `rotationHigh`
   118→
   119→### Stop Loss
   120→- Below/above the newly established rotation levels
   121→- These are the levels from the breakout candle
   122→
   123→### Take Profit
   124→- Previous significant swing
   125→- Measured move (height of previous rotation range)
   126→- 2:1 minimum risk-reward
   127→
   128→### Aggressive vs Conservative Entry
   129→
   130→| Approach | Entry Point | Risk | Reward Potential |
   131→|----------|-------------|------|------------------|
   132→| Aggressive | Immediately on color flip | Higher | Larger move captured |
   133→| Conservative | On first pullback after flip | Lower | Smaller but higher probability |
   134→
   135→### Why This Works
   136→You're entering exactly when structure shifts. Most traders are still positioned for the old direction—you're ahead of the crowd.

   140→## Playbook 3: Swing Failure Pattern (SFP)
   141→
   142→### The Concept
   143→Fade false breakouts when price wicks beyond a rotation level but closes back inside. The indicator highlights these in ORANGE.
   144→
   145→### Entry Rules
   146→
   147→**Long SFP:**
   148→1. Price wicks BELOW `rotationLow`
   149→2. Price closes ABOVE `rotationLow` (same candle)
   150→3. Level turns ORANGE (false breakout detected)
   151→4. Enter long on candle close
   152→5. Stop below the wick low
   153→
   154→**Short SFP:**
   155→1. Price wicks ABOVE `rotationHigh`
   156→2. Price closes BELOW `rotationHigh` (same candle)
   157→3. Level turns ORANGE (false breakout detected)
   158→4. Enter short on candle close
   159→5. Stop above the wick high
   160→
   161→### Stop Loss
   162→- Just beyond the false breakout wick
   163→- This is the tightest stop of all playbooks
   164→
   165→### Take Profit
   166→- Opposite rotation level
   167→- Previous swing in the reversal direction
   168→- Minimum 2:1 risk-reward (often get 3:1+)
   169→
   170→### Why This Works
   171→
   172→**Liquidity Grab Dynamics:**
   173→```
   174→1. Obvious level visible (rotation high/low)
   175→2. Retail traders place stops just beyond
   176→3. Smart money pushes price through to trigger stops
   177→4. Stops triggered = liquidity for large orders
   178→5. Price reverses back into range
   179→6. Indicator shows ORANGE = trap confirmed
   180→```
   181→
   182→### Best SFP Setups
   183→- Orange highlight at prior significant S/R
   184→- High volume on the false breakout candle
   185→- Long wick relative to body
   186→- Occurs after extended move (exhaustion)

   190→## Playbook 4: Invalidation Trade (Counter-Trend)
   191→
   192→### The Concept
   193→Trade when rotation is invalidated (gray bars) as an early reversal signal. Higher risk but catches turns early.
   194→
   195→### Entry Rules
   196→
   197→1. Price was in confirmed rotation (green or red)
   198→2. Price crosses opposite level WITHOUT meeting full rotation criteria
   199→3. Bars turn GRAY (invalidation)
   200→4. Wait for confirmation candle in new direction
   201→5. Enter on confirmation
   202→
   203→### What Invalidation Means
   204→
   205→```
   206→Was in UPWARD rotation (green):
   207→  → Price drops below rotationLow
   208→  → But doesn't close below AND below low[1]
   209→  → Bars turn GRAY
   210→  → Structure is broken but new trend not confirmed
   211→```
   212→
   213→### Stop Loss
   214→- Beyond the recent swing that caused invalidation
   215→- Wider stop required (structure unclear)
   216→
   217→### Take Profit
   218→- Modest targets (structure uncertain)
   219→- Scale out in thirds
   220→- Move to breakeven quickly
   221→
   222→### Risk Management
   223→- **Reduce position size** for this playbook (50% of normal)
   224→- Requires additional confluence:
   225→  - MTF showing opposite direction
   226→  - Volume divergence
   227→  - Key reversal level (daily/weekly S/R)
   228→  - RSI divergence
   229→
   230→### When to Skip
   231→- If invalidation is just noise in a strong trend
   232→- No confluence factors present
   233→- Low volume environment

   237→## Playbook 5: Multi-Timeframe Confluence
   238→
   239→### The Concept
   240→Only trade when chart timeframe AND higher timeframe rotations align. Dramatically reduces false signals.
   241→
   242→### Setup Requirements
   243→
   244→Enable MTF in indicator settings:
   245→- Enable Multi-Timeframe Levels: `true`
   246→- Timeframe: Higher than chart (e.g., 4H for 15min chart, Daily for 1H chart)
   247→
   248→### Entry Rules
   249→
   250→**High Probability Longs:**
   251→1. Chart timeframe bars are GREEN
   252→2. MTF rotation is also bullish (price above MTF rotationLow)
   253→3. Blue MTF levels sloping up or flat (not breaking down)
   254→4. Enter using Playbook 1, 2, or 3 criteria
   255→
   256→**High Probability Shorts:**
   257→1. Chart timeframe bars are RED
   258→2. MTF rotation is also bearish (price below MTF rotationHigh)
   259→3. Aqua MTF levels sloping down or flat (not breaking up)
   260→4. Enter using Playbook 1, 2, or 3 criteria
   261→
   262→### Alignment Matrix
   263→
   264→| Chart TF | MTF Direction | Action |
   265→|----------|---------------|--------|
   266→| GREEN | Bullish | ✅ LONG - High probability |
   267→| RED | Bearish | ✅ SHORT - High probability |
   268→| GREEN | Bearish | ⚠️ CAUTION - Counter-trend, reduce size |
   269→| RED | Bullish | ⚠️ CAUTION - Counter-trend, reduce size |
   270→| GRAY | Any | ⏸️ WAIT - No clear structure |
   271→
   272→### Stop Loss
   273→- Use MTF rotation levels for stops (wider but more significant)
   274→- OR use chart TF levels with reduced size
   275→
   276→### Why This Works
   277→- Trades aligned with "the big picture"
   278→- Avoids getting chopped up in noise
   279→- Higher timeframe structure rarely fails without warning

   283→## Playbook 6: Breakout and Retest
   284→
   285→### The Concept
   286→Wait for rotation to confirm, then enter on the retest of the broken level. Reduces false breakout risk.
   287→
   288→### Entry Rules
   289→
   290→**For Longs:**
   291→1. Rotation flips GREEN (breakout above rotationHigh confirmed)
   292→2. WAIT for price to pull back to old `rotationHigh`
   293→3. Old resistance now acts as support
   294→4. Enter when retest holds (bullish candle)
   295→
   296→**For Shorts:**
   297→1. Rotation flips RED (breakdown below rotationLow confirmed)
   298→2. WAIT for price to pull back to old `rotationLow`
   299→3. Old support now acts as resistance
   300→4. Enter when retest holds (bearish candle)
   301→
   302→### Stop Loss
   303→- Just beyond the retest level
   304→- If it fails, the breakout was likely false
   305→
   306→### Take Profit
   307→- Project the range height from breakout point
   308→- Previous significant swing
   309→- Trail with new rotation levels
   310→
   311→### Entry Timing
   312→
   313→```
   314→BREAKOUT SEQUENCE:
   315→━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
   316→
   317→1. [Gray bars] ────── Ranging
   318→2. [Breakout candle] ─ Green flip, breaks above rotationHigh
   319→3. [Rally] ─────────── Price moves up (DON'T CHASE)
   320→4. [Pullback] ──────── Price returns to old rotationHigh
   321→5. [Retest hold] ───── Bullish candle at old resistance
   322→6. [ENTRY] ─────────── Enter long here ← OPTIMAL ENTRY
   323→```
   324→
   325→### Why This Works
   326→- Confirms the breakout was genuine (level flips from resistance to support)
   327→- Better entry price than chasing
   328→- Clear invalidation level (if retest fails)
   329→- Often get to see volume characteristics on retest

   333→## Playbook 7: Range Trading (Gray Bar Strategy)
   334→
   335→### The Concept
   336→When bars are gray (invalidation), trade the range between rotation levels.
   337→
   338→### Entry Rules
   339→
   340→**Prerequisites:**
   341→1. Bars must be GRAY (no confirmed direction)
   342→2. Clear `rotationHigh` and `rotationLow` levels visible
   343→3. Range is wide enough to trade (at least 2 ATR)
   344→
   345→**Long at Range Bottom:**
   346→1. Price approaches `rotationLow`
   347→2. Bullish candle pattern forms
   348→3. Enter long with stop below the low
   349→4. Target: `rotationHigh` or middle of range
   350→
   351→**Short at Range Top:**
   352→1. Price approaches `rotationHigh`
   353→2. Bearish candle pattern forms
   354→3. Enter short with stop above the high
   355→4. Target: `rotationLow` or middle of range
   356→
   357→### Critical Rule
   358→**EXIT ALL POSITIONS IMMEDIATELY when bars turn GREEN or RED.**
   359→
   360→If rotation confirms while you're in a range trade against the direction, you will get run over.
   361→
   362→### Stop Loss
   363→- Just beyond the range edge
   364→- Tight stops required (small range = small stops)
   365→
   366→### Position Sizing
   367→- Reduce size in ranges (choppier, less directional)
   368→- Multiple small wins > one big loss
   369→
   370→### When to Avoid
   371→- Bars flickering between colors (unstable)
   372→- News events approaching
   373→- Very tight range (less than 1 ATR)
   374→- Been in range for extended period (breakout coming)

   437→## Common Mistakes to Avoid
   438→
   439→### 1. Chasing Rotation Flips
   440→**Wrong:** Entering 5 bars after green flip when price already moved
   441→**Right:** Enter on the flip bar or wait for retest (Playbook 6)
   442→
   443→### 2. Fighting MTF Direction
   444→**Wrong:** Going long on 15min green when 4H is red
   445→**Right:** Align with higher timeframe or reduce size significantly
   446→
   447→### 3. Ignoring Orange Warnings
   448→**Wrong:** Staying long after orange highlight on rotationHigh
   449→**Right:** Recognize potential SFP, tighten stop or take profits
   450→
   451→### 4. Trading Gray Bars Directionally
   452→**Wrong:** Taking trend trades when bars are gray
   453→**Right:** Either range trade (Playbook 7) or wait for direction
   454→
   455→### 5. No Stop Loss
   456→**Wrong:** "The rotation will hold"
   457→**Right:** If price closes beyond rotation level with confirmation, you're wrong—exit
   458→
   459→### 6. Overtrading Choppy Markets
   460→**Wrong:** Taking every rotation flip in ranging market
   461→**Right:** If bars flip colors frequently (>3 times in 20 bars), stand aside
